Dungeon Keeper

Created by Edward Li

NOTE: The game may take a minute or two to load, depending on your internet speed.

Check out the source code on Github

Download the game (or download the terminal version)

Controls:

  • [W] [A] [S] [D] to move
  • Hover hover elements on screen to see details
  • Walk into enemies to attack
  • Walk over an item and press [Q] to pick up or [X] to delete
  • Press [E] to open your inventory
  • Once in inventory, click on an item to click/use, or press [Q] to drop or [X] to delete
  • Press [E] or [ESC] to leave the inventory

Instructions:

Kill the dungeon keeper, obtain the dungeon key, and leave the dungeon!

Enemies:

  • Guards do basic attack and defense. They have chance of dropping armor, weapon, or potions.
  • Wisps always tries to follow the player. On death, they drop ectoplasm and spawn whispers.
  • Whispers have fast movement and decent attack.
  • Mages have small attack, but have large defense and spawns damaging fire. They drop the mage's robe.
  • Vampires have large attack and health, but little defense. They drop vampire fangs.
  • Dungeon Keepers are the final bosses. They have large attack, health, and defense. They drop the dungeon key.

Items:

  • Potions give various permanent buffs, such as health, defense, or attack.
  • Guard's weapons and swords are the basic armors and weapons.
  • Guard's weapons and swords are the basic armors and weapons.
  • Ectoplasm spawns Soul Fire, which do not despawn and only damages enemies.
  • Dungeon key unlocks the dungeon exit, finishing the game.

Tiles:

  • Spikes damage you when stepped on.
  • Fire only damages players.
  • Soul Fire only damages enemies.

Cheats + Debug:

  • Press [F10] to enable god mode
  • Press [F11] to enable rendering test mode
  • Press [F12] to enable debug mode
